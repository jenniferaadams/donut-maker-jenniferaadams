Welcome to Farm Clicker! We run an organic farm that grows vegetables!

To play the game, start clicking on the vegetables in the center of the screen. Each time you click, you grow more vegetables!

To grow vegetables faster, you can buy a tractor. With a tractor, you earn more vegetables per click. More tractors equals more vegetables on each click!

You can also add a farmer. A farmer will automatically grow vegetables for you! Each farmer will grow one vegetable per second.

The more farmers and tractors you collect, the more vegetables you will be able to harvest!
